## The Supplier Performance Group

The Supplier Performance Group exists to help Suppliers in the Industrial and Construction marketplaces thrive and prosper.
<br>
We write about all aspects of the supplier experience — working with GPOs and buying groups, managing independent manufacturer's reps, trade shows, sales force structure, channel management, and upstream supplier relationships.
