## The Supplier Performance Group

A note on terminology

## Putting Suppliers on Equal Footing

I love buying groups, but let's face it -- they are

- **first**, for the distributor members, 
- **second**, for the group itself, and 
- **third**, for the suppliers.

I want to be clear that this is not a knock on the groups, or on the above-mentioned "order of importance."  The groups I know of have never been shy about communicating this.  Yes, maybe it's not stressed, but it's certainly understood.  And it's evident in the structure and programs of the group.
