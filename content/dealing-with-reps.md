Many small-to-midsize manufacturers have one or two dedicated sales team members, but depend on independent manufacturers reps to sell products out in the field.

Dealing with these Reps is an art and a skill in-and-of itself. 

They are knowledgeable, and very independent.  But interesting them in your line, getting them to talk about *your line* and *your products* during a customer visit, when they represent any number of suppliers, can be a challenge.
